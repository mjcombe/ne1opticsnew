About NE1 Optics

A local practice with a personal touch

NE1 Optics was founded with a simple belief: quality optical care should be personal, unhurried, and genuinely focused on the individual. From our location on Northumberland Street, we've been serving Newcastle for over two decades.

As an independent practice, we're not bound by corporate targets or brand partnerships. This means our advice is always honest, our recommendations are always in your interest, and our service is always personal.

We're proud to be a trusted part of the Newcastle community — known not just for our expertise, but for the warmth and care we bring to every single interaction.
